A Few Of My Favorite Things

I am a Graphics Editor and cartographer at Natonal Geographic, with a B.S. in Geography from Penn State and an M.S. in Cartography from the University of Wisconsin, Madison. I am also a former board member for the North American Cartographic Information Society (NACIS) and organizer of Maptime D.C. I was born and raised in southern Rhode Island and enjoy exploring my current home, Washington, D.C.

Here's a few more things about me...

  • I played rugby for 13 years and won the Division I Collegiate National Championship in 2004

  • I love to travel (like a true geographer) and have been to 18 countries on 5 continents, as well as 37 states and territories!

  • My favorite trip was 3 weeks spent in New Zealand touring both the North & South Islands. This country truly is a geographer's paradise and I can't wait to go back!

  • I have a puppy named Lola. She keeps life exciting and reminds me not to work too long on my computer!

  • My homestate is Rhode Island and it's small size but unique qualities are what inspired me to become a geographer!
