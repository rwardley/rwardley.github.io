A Few Of My Favorite Things

I am a cartographer by trade and a geographer by heart. I love exploring the world through my work at National Geographic and by traveling when I get the opportunity. I have been extremely fortunate to learn from and work with many giants in our field over the past decade, from my undergraduate studies at Penn State to my current masters work at UW Madison. I am a firm believer in knowledge sharing & collaboration which is why I help organize our local D.C. chapter of Maptime and serve as a board member of NACIS. In my spare time I enjoy playing esoteric sports, spending time in the great outdoors, and accumulating cultural knowledge in one of the many museums in Washington, D.C.

  • I played Division I rugby throughout college and won a National Championship in 2004

  • I love to travel (like a true geographer) and have been to at least 1 country on 5 continents

  • My favorite trip was to Salzburg, Austria where we toured the Sound of Music sites and took the most amazing hike in the Salzkammergut (the photo on my homepage is from that hike!)

  • I have a puppy named Lola. She keeps life exciting and reminds me not to work too long on my computer!

  • My homestate is Rhode Island and it's small size but unique qualities are what inspired me to become a geographer!
